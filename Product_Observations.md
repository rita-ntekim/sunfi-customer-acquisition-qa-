# Product Observations

## Project

Quality Assessment of SunFi's Customer Acquisition Journey

---

## Purpose

This document captures usability observations and product improvement opportunities identified during manual testing. These are not software defects but recommendations that could improve the customer experience and support lead conversion.

---

## OBS-001

### Observation

The exit feedback survey appears too early in the customer journey.

### Impact

Users may be interrupted before they have had enough time to evaluate available solar solutions, increasing cognitive load and disrupting exploration.

### Recommendation

Trigger the survey based on stronger exit intent signals (for example, after meaningful inactivity or when the user attempts to leave the page).

---

## OBS-002

### Observation

The business and NGO purchase journey contains many steps, making the process feel lengthy for first-time users.

### Impact

A long onboarding flow may increase abandonment before lead submission.

### Recommendation

Consider introducing a progress indicator, reducing unnecessary steps, or allowing users to save progress.

---

## OBS-003

### Observation

The landing page feels visually crowded because the enquiry form competes with the hero section for attention.

### Impact

First-time visitors may struggle to quickly understand SunFi's value proposition before being asked to complete a form.

### Recommendation

Create more visual separation between onboarding content and the lead capture form.

---

## OBS-004

### Observation

The product catalogue displays many options simultaneously, making it difficult for new users to compare solutions.

### Impact

High cognitive load may slow decision-making.

### Recommendation

Consider progressive disclosure, better categorisation, or guided filtering.

---

## OBS-005

### Observation

The recommendation generation message indicates that results will take "a second," but actual generation takes noticeably longer.

### Impact

The mismatch between system feedback and actual performance may reduce user trust.

### Recommendation

Display a more realistic loading message or provide a progress indicator during recommendation generation.

---

## OBS-006

### Observation

The "Day Usage" and "Night Usage" fields in the Build My Own System flow are not sufficiently explained for first-time users.

### Impact

Users may be unsure what information is required, increasing the likelihood of incorrect inputs or abandonment.

### Recommendation

Provide contextual help, examples, or tooltips to guide users.

---

## OBS-007

### Observation

The mobile experience appears visually dense, with limited spacing between interface elements.

### Impact

The interface feels crowded on smaller screens and may reduce readability.

### Recommendation

Review spacing, hierarchy, and responsive layouts for mobile devices.
