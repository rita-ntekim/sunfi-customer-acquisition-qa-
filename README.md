# SunFi Customer Acquisition Journey - Manual QA Assessment

## Overview

This repository documents a manual Quality Assurance (QA) assessment of SunFi's customer acquisition journey.

The objective of this assessment was to evaluate the lead generation experience from a customer's perspective by identifying functional defects, validating core workflows, and documenting usability observations that could improve the overall product experience.

The assessment focuses on the complete customer acquisition flow—from initiating a solar enquiry to receiving a tailored recommendation.

---

## Scope

The following areas were assessed during testing:

- Customer acquisition journey
- Lead generation workflow
- Form validation
- Navigation behaviour
- Customer support accessibility
- Recommendation flow
- Cross-browser behaviour
- Mobile responsiveness
- General usability

## Key Findings

The core customer acquisition flow is functional and allows users to complete the lead generation process successfully.

During testing, a small number of functional and UI defects were identified, primarily affecting navigation, customer support accessibility, and interaction within parts of the recommendation flow. In addition, several usability observations were documented to highlight opportunities for improving clarity, reducing friction, and enhancing the overall customer experience.
